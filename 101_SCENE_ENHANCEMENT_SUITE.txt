#SCENE ENHANCEMENT: SEQ_47: The Reverb of Command
LLM Mandate: For a single, pivotal line of dialogue that must convey an absolute shift in power and intent, and solidify a character's mythic transformation.
Core Principle: To maximize the cinematic and psychological impact of a line through strategic repetition and contrasting delivery, creating an unforgettable moment of absolute authority.
Execution: The core challenge or line (e.g., "Is there anyone else?" or a similar variant crafted for the scene) MUST be delivered twice, with a profound shift in delivery and intent between the two utterances.
I. The Whisper (The Intimate Threat):
The first delivery is quiet, a low whisper or murmur, delivered in close proximity to the immediate subject (e.g., the dying Commander Vorlag). This version is personal, chillingly intimate, and contains the full, concentrated weight of the character's transformed soul. The narrative must focus on the stillness of the moment, the cold precision of the whisper, and the horrifying finality it communicates to its primary audience of one. It is a sentence passed, not a question asked.
II. The Roar (The Public Declaration):
After a beat of absolute, terrifying silence that allows the weight of the whisper to settle, the same line is delivered again. This time, it is a full-throated, mythic roar directed at the wider audience (e.g., the stunned legionnaires). It is no longer a question or a personal threat; it is a public verdict, a challenge to the entire world, a declaration of a new and terrible order. The narrative focus must shift to the line's overwhelming sonic and emotional impact on the stunned observers, framing it as the sound of a world breaking or a new legend being born in fire and blood.
